Lash lift and tint

A small amount of the product is applied on the back of the ear using a micro applicator. This has to be done atleast 24 hours before the treatment to give time for a reaction to take place. Avoiding touching the area, or washing the area for 24 hours. If a reaction takes place, the are will look red or blotchy. Signs of a reactions are area going hot, itching, swelling and burning.

You can either book in for 2-3 week top up on the day of your apt when you leave or you can call the clinic, or book in a slot online- if doing this I would recommend ideally atleast 1 weeks prior to your apt to ensure that you get a slot long enough for your top up.

A lash lift lasts between 6-8 weeks. Lashes will slowly start to drop back down to their original position after this time.

Brow and lash tints last between 2-3 weeks

Hi brows last around 3-4 weeks. This is when you I’ll start to no time hair growth around the brows again and tint will start to wear off.

Avoid getting wet for 24 hours after the treatment

Avoid steam rooms and saunas for 48 hours

Do not use lash perming or lash curlers at all

Do not sleep on your face

Do not wear mascara

Clean lashes every evening with lash cleanser and lash brush

Brush lashes daily from route to tip of lashes (I will supply you with a lash brush at your appointment)

Use non oil based products on your face and keep moistures away from your lashes

Do not run or pick your extensions

I would recommend purchasing a lash cleanser, I can recommend products to you at your appointment to use.

Lash cleanser often come with a very misfit and gentle brush to clean your lashes with, gently cleanse your lashes by putting a small lump of the cleanser on the back of your hand, dipping your brush in the cleanser and ever so gently from the tip of the lashes to the bottom brush through.

Use a pad to pat dry very gently and then brush through with a dry lash spooly, that I will give to you at your appointment.

Doing this will not make your lashes fall out but will improve retention and keep your lashes clean from bacteria and makeup build up.

-Come with no makeup of moisturisers on.

-Come in with an idea of how you want your eyelashes to look

-Come in with no eyelashes on from another tech, I cannot infil other lash tech’s lashes. They will need to be removed either by me or by the previous tech. If you require your lashes removed before your appointment, please let the clinic know, as this will require more time for your appointment.
