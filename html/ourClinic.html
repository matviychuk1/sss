AESTHETICS PRACTICE

REVIVE, ENHANCE, RESTORE.

MAE cosmetics is a Leading beauty & Aesthetics practice that have successfully treated over 4,000 clients with over 250 5 star reviews.

We are passionate about helping people look and feel their best. Mae Cosmetics & Wellness combine treatments that improve emotional and physical wellbeing.

We believe that looking good and feeling good go hand in hand and you cant have one without the other. MAE offer a variety of treatments that help you feel your best from the outside in and the inside out!
We are an innovative Practice with overall client wellbeing at our core. As we have grown within the Beauty Industry we believed that a core part of looking good was to do with how a client feels from within.
Thats why we don’t only offer Aesthetic Treatments but Holistic treatments aswell as supporting online groups and events. We offer a wide range of services with our specialist staff members including Beauty & Aesthetic Treatments such as dermal fillers and permanent make up.
Aswell as Holistic services such as Acupuncture, Cupping, Cosmetic Acupuncture, online womens circles and wellbeing events.Our practitioners have the knowledge, experience & passion to create a look that is personal, natural & complimentary to each individual client, that contributes to their appearance & self confidence.
We treat our clients on a personal & individual basis, creating a look that is bespoke to them.

Master one

her position

Danielle decided to train as a cosmetic tattoo artist after having a successful treatment herself. The rise in her self-esteem gave her the inspiration to begin her career in the Beauty and Aesthetics industry and founded Mae cosmetics in 2016. Danielle went on to complete all of her Aesthetic qualifications and now holds the highest qualification of a Level 7 Aesthetic Practitioner.

'The ethos behind my clinic is that of creating a safe yet relaxed environment to deliver high end clinical treatments for clients in a professional manner.'
